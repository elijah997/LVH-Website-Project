Main Restaurant

The Lakeside Buffet Restaurant

Our flagship buffet restaurant offers an extraordinary culinary journey through international flavours and authentic Ugandan cuisine. Each meal is a celebration of fresh, locally sourced ingredients prepared by our award-winning culinary team.

Enjoy sweeping views of the hotel gardens and Lake Victoria while you dine. The warm ambiance, attentive service, and an ever-rotating menu make every visit a new discovery.

The Bar

The Lakeview Bar

Settle into a comfortable chair at our stylish Lakeview Bar and let the beauty of Lake Victoria wash over you. Our expert mixologists craft inspired cocktails using local spirits and fresh tropical ingredients.

Whether you prefer a post-dinner nightcap, a refreshing sundowner with colleagues, or a quiet space to unwind — the Lakeview Bar sets the perfect scene.
